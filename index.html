About Steve Universe

I’m Justice Muhammed Okan, writing as Stephen Muhammed, from Abuja, Nigeria. Since age 16, I’ve crafted Afrofuturistic tales to fill the gap in African sci-fi. With a degree in Educational Administration from the University of Abuja, I teach while dreaming up vibrant futures.

My mission is to ignite a universe of interconnected African stories, starting with Pishon: The Uprising and beyond.
