The Flare

In the neon-lit streets of Wellrose, a city teetering on the edge of chaos, a fiery crash in 2100 unleashes the Neon Stone—an alien artifact that ignites a war between heroes and monsters. Alex Rosicky, a grieving son resurrected by its power, rises as The Flare, a lightning-fueled vigilante fighting to save a city that fears him. Alongside his team, the Paragon, he battles rogue superhumans, a ruthless corporation, and a sister lost to darkness. As Genesys Corp tightens its grip and an ancient evil awakens, Alex must confront his past and unite a frac-tured world to stop a catastrophe that threatens all of humanity. Within The Dark.

It was not a free world. Bloodshed. Slavery. Silence. The Church ruled with an iron scripture, branding obedience as holiness and truth as heresy. The people lived in shadows, chained by ignorance—a design by the Supreme Church to keep them docile. They were told the Church possessed all knowledge, all divine authority. And so, they dared not speak. They dared not dream. But legends have a way of surviving—even when hope dies. Whispers spoke of a Chosen One, destined to lead humanity into light, to restore free will, to bring forth true worship. Yet decades passed. No saviour came. The people’s faith rotted into apathy. Hope was now a tale mothers told their children to help them sleep through the screams. But even in the deepest dark... a spark still burned.

About Me

I'm Justice Muhammed Okan, writing as Steve Universe, hailing from Abuja, FCT, Nigeria, where I started scribbling tales at 16, driven by the glaring gap in Afrofuturistic stories on the shelves. With a degree in Educational Administration and Planning from the University of Abuja, I now teach in a secondary school, dreaming up worlds between lesson plans.

I am fueled by a mission to flood the market with African futures--and it's just the first spark in a blazing universe of interconnected books.
